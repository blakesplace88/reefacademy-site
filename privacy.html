About Reef Academy

Reef Academy is an educational app designed to help children in Pre-Kindergarten through 4th grade learn math, reading, science, and writing through fun, interactive games.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Reef Academy is designed for use by children under the age of 13. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from children. This includes names, email addresses, location data, or any other identifying information.

What Data We Store

All app data — including your child's name, grade level, lesson progress, and pearl count — is stored only on this device. It is never transmitted to any server, shared with any third party, or used for any purpose other than displaying your child's progress within the app.

No Advertising

Reef Academy contains no advertising of any kind. We do not use advertising networks, behavioral tracking, or any third-party analytics services that could collect data about your child.

No Third-Party Services

Reef Academy does not integrate with any social networks, external websites, or third-party data services. The app functions entirely offline after download.

Subscriptions

Reef Academy offers an optional premium subscription for parents. Subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store and are subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Parents can manage or cancel subscriptions at any time through iPhone/iPad Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Parental Controls

The Parent Dashboard is protected by a parental gate to prevent children from accessing parent-only features, including subscription management and progress reports.

Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling Reef Academy from your device.
